Welcome to KP-Korea.org!

Developing software under the principles of Juche since 2019.

In memory of our Supreme Leaders - Kim Il Sung and Kim Jong Il



Juche - the political ideology of North Korea, which aims to achieve national autonomy through the rule of a single leader, the maintenance of a strong military, and the pursuit of economic self-sufficiency.


Welcome to KP-Korea.org, a place where self-reliance meets programming. This website hosts a collection of various projects built independently and guided by Juche ideology. This means that there are no teams or corporations behind the projects presented here - just independent development at its finest.
